Rice Recipe

Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of rice
  • 2 cups of water
  • 1 tablespoon of butter or oil
  • 1 teaspoon of salt

Instructions:

  1. Rinse the rice under cold water until the water runs clear.
  2. In a medium saucepan, melt the butter over medium heat.
  3. Add the rice and stir for a few minutes until the rice is lightly toasted.
  4. Add water and salt, and bring to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, reduce the heat to low, cover, and let it simmer for about 15-18 minutes until the water is absorbed.
  6. Remove from heat and let the rice sit, covered, for an additional 5 minutes.
  7. Fluff the rice with a fork before serving.
